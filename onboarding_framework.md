# CrackedMilk: Client Onboarding & Strategy Framework

This framework is our "Secret Sauce" for transforming struggling products into high-growth engines. We implement this for every product we onboard.

## 1. The "Painkiller" Audit
Before we start any marketing, we audit the product-market fit. We strictly follow the "Kill the Vitamin" philosophy.

*   **Painkiller Products:** Solve problems where the customer faces major negative consequences if left unsolved (loss of money, legal issues, wasted time). These are typically used daily/weekly and are B2B focused.
*   **Vitamin Products:** Solve minor problems (fewer likes, social habits). These are "nice to have" and often struggle to scale.

**Our Rule:** If we identify a product as a "Vitamin," our first task is to find the "Painkiller" angle or pivot the positioning before launching the AI Video Growth Engine.

## 2. The Core 7 Strategy Questions
We require every client (and our internal team) to answer these 7 baseline questions before execution:
1.  **What one specific problem am I solving?**
2.  **What audience segment craves this solution the most?**
3.  **What is their current go-to solution (the status quo)?**
4.  **What is good and bad about that current solution?**
5.  **How is this product undeniably better than the alternatives?**
6.  **What pricing is a "no-brainer" for the audience but profitable for the client?**
7.  **How will we get the first 10 (or next 100) customers?**

## 3. The Landing Page "Surgical" Fix
Our AI Video Engine drives traffic, but the landing page must convert it. We implement these high-conversion standards:
*   **You-Focused Copy:** Replace "we/our" with "you/your." Focus on what the customer *gets*, not what the product *is*.
*   **The Promise + Explanation:** Every heading must be an emotional promise + a rational explanation of how it's delivered.
*   **Social Proof First:** Aggressive placement of testimonials and "Founder Messages" to build trust.
*   **Visual Desire:** High-end visuals and "Loom-style" demo videos are mandatory.
*   **The "Roast" Protocol:** We jump on calls with 30 target users who have never seen the page and watch them use it. The feedback is often brutal, but it's how we double conversion rates.

## 4. Multi-Channel Beyond the Launch
The "Product Hunt" high is temporary. We build a sustainable mix:
*   **Primary:** AI Video Growth Engine (TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
*   **Secondary:**
    *   **Cold Outreach:** Manual, sincere DMs/Emails for B2B.
    *   **Free SEO Tools:** Small, high-utility tools to win search intent.
    *   **Programmatic SEO:** Data-driven pages, not AI spam.
    *   **Newsletter Nurturing:** Building a weekly connection with the audience.

## 5. The Iteration Mindset
Nothing works on the first try. We operate in weekly sprints:
*   **Pick ONE goal per week:** (e.g., "Sharpen positioning" or "Increase landing page conversion").
*   **Sprints:** Audit -> Generate -> Test -> Iterate.
*   **Fail Fast:** If a channel or hook doesn't work in 7 days, we pivot.
